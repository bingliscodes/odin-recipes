Beef and Tofu Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a Trader Joe's delicacy. Using a combination of beef and tofu, this creates for a delicuous and nutritious and cost-friendly meal!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 Trader Joe's marinated Bulkogi Beef, thinly sliced
  • 2 packs of Trader Joe's high protein tofu
  • 3 bags Trader Joe's vegetable fried rice

Steps

  1. Crush the tofu. Put something really heavy on it for about 2 hours to get the excess moisture out
  2. Make the bulgogi marinade (ask Valerie, something to do with fuji apples)
  3. Cut tofu into small cubes
  4. Marinade tofu (at least 4 hours, overnight ideally)
  5. Cook the beef, tofu, and fried rice
  6. Portion and serve
