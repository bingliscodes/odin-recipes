Carne en su Jugo

Description

Carne en su jugo is a Guadalajara favorite. Small pieces of flank steak are cooked in their juices, then mixed with whole beans and crispy crumbled bacon. It makes me feel at home!

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds beef, thinly sliced
  • 1 Pack cooked bacon
  • 12 small or 7 large Tomatillos
  • 15 cilantros
  • 2 diente de Ajo
  • 8 cebollitas
  • 1 Cebolla
  • 1.5 pinch comino
  • 1.5 bote de frijole
  • 2 cans sweet corn (optional)

Steps

  1. 1. Make the salsa by combining the cilantro, tomatillos, ajo, and cebolla
  2. 2. Cook the beef and cebollitas
  3. 3. Combine salsa with cooked beef. Simmer for 20 minutes
  4. 4. Add cooked bacon, frijoles, and corn
  5. 5. Portion and serve
